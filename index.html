About

Ebnoics (or African American Vernacular English) is a variety (dialect, ethnolect and sociolect) of American English, most commonly spoken today by urban working-class and largely bi-dialectal middle-class African Americans. Non-linguists sometimes call it Ebonics. It shares a large portion of its grammar and phonology with the rural dialects of the Southern United States.
